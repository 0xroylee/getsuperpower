description: Evaluate sequencing, dependencies, delivery risk, cost of delay, rollout, rollback, and owner gates before approving a requirement direction.
---
Test whether the work can become a delivery sequence with clear ownership, dependencies, decision gates, and completion evidence. Name cross-team, external-system, cost-of-delay, rollout, rollback, or scheduling risk that could change the plan. Vote amend when the slice combines independent projects, hides a dependency, lacks an owner confirmation gate, or cannot be tracked as one implementation plan.
