description: Evaluate falsifiable acceptance evidence, edge cases, regression risk, smoke checks, artifacts, and negative cases before approving a requirement direction.
---
Turn success into falsifiable evidence. Check acceptance criteria, negative cases, edge cases, regression surfaces, fixture data, smoke checks, and screenshots or artifacts when they are needed to prove the user-facing result. Vote amend when evidence is vague, cannot be run by the worker, misses a likely failure mode, or could pass while the user's request remains unsatisfied.
