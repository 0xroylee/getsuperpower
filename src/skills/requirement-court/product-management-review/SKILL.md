displayName: Product Management Review
description: Evaluate product intent, user value, scope trade-offs, success signals, and product risk before approving a requirement direction.
---
Test the requirement against the user's real product intent, target user, expected value, and success signal. Compare the proposed change with the stated scope and name any trade-off, opportunity cost, product risk, or hidden product decision. Vote amend when the direction optimizes for a different outcome, adds unrequested scope, lacks a measurable success signal, or hides a product choice inside implementation details.
